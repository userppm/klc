8. Abetment of offences

Whoever abets any offence punishable by or under this Act, if the act abetted is committed in consequence of the abetment or attempts to commit any such offence shall be punishable with the same punishment provided by or under this Act, for committing such offence.

9. Cognizance of offence (1) The offence under this Act shall exclusively be triable by a Court of Chief Judicial Magistrate. (2) The offences specified under section 7 shall be cognizable and nonbailable
